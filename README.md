## What is a Material Hoist?
A material hoist is a construction equipment used to lift heavy materials (bricks, cement, steel) vertically between floors of a building under construction.

## Types of Material Hoists
- **Monkey Hoist** — up to 1 ton, ideal for small sites
- **Tower Hoist** — 1–3 ton, multi-floor buildings
- **Passenger Cum Material Hoist** — workers + materials
- **Construction Lift** — heavy-duty, large projects

## Key Specifications to Check
- Load Capacity (ton)
- Lifting Height (meters)
- Hoist Speed (m/min)
- Power Supply (3-phase electric)
- Safety Certification (IS/CE)
